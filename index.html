Basic Info

The red eye tree frogs ar medium size frogs that reside in a variety of places from swamps, wooded swamps, ponds, lakes, marshes etc... They are nocturnal animals so they hunt and thrive at night. Their natural life span can go on for about 4-5 years.

Red eye tree frogs are carinvores so they mostly eat insects. These inscets range from crickets, grasshoppers, flies, and moths. Sometimes they will even eat smaller frogs, oh noo!
